SCIENTIFIC BASIS:
================================================================================
All calculations based on peer-reviewed research:

- Widmark Equation: E.M.P. Widmark (1932), validated in 1000+ studies
- Distribution Ratios: Lieber (2005), Ramchandani et al. (2001)
- Elimination Rates: Wilkinson (2005), Jones (2010)
- Food Absorption: Sedman et al. (1976), Chatterjee et al. (1998)
- Impairment Levels: NHTSA, Moskowitz & Fiorentino (2000)
- Tennessee DUI: HB 1414/SB 1117 (effective July 1, 2024)

HOW TO USE:
================================================================================

INSTALLATION:
1. Download the BAC_Simulator.app folder
2. Place on Desktop or in Applications folder
3. Double-click to launch

BASIC WORKFLOW:
1. App opens with welcome message
2. Chat with bot to provide profile (sex, weight, age, etc.)
3. Tell bot about drinks consumed and food eaten
4. View real-time BAC calculation and timeline
5. Update scenario as new drinks/food consumed

EXAMPLE CONVERSATION:
   Bot: "What's your biological sex?"
   You: "male"
   Bot: "What's your weight?"
   You: "180 lbs"
   Bot: "What's your height?"
   You: "6 feet"
   Bot: "How old are you?"
   You: "30"
   Bot: "Do you drink regularly?"
   You: "no, rarely"
   Bot: "When did you start drinking?"
   You: "7pm"

   You: "I had pizza at 6pm, then 2 beers at 7pm and 8pm"
