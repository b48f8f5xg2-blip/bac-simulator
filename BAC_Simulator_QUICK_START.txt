WHAT IS THIS?
A desktop app that calculates your Blood Alcohol Content (BAC) based on what
you've eaten and drunk. It shows you real-time impairment levels and whether
you're legally safe to drive.

KEY TIPS:
================================================================================

1. FOOD MATTERS A LOT
   - Empty stomach = BAC peaks FAST (30-60 min)
   - Full meal = BAC peaks SLOWER (90+ min)
   - Pizza/fatty food = Biggest delay (can reduce peak by 50-75%)
   - This is real science: food delays stomach emptying

2. NATURAL LANGUAGE WORKS
   The bot understands casual language:
   ‚úì "I had a beer"
   ‚úì "Drank whiskey"
   ‚úì "Just ate pizza"
   ‚úì "Mixed drink, 40% vodka"
   ‚úì "Light beer, 12 ounces"
   ‚úì "Started drinking 2 hours ago"

3. YOU CAN UPDATE IN REAL-TIME
   Just tell the bot:
   - "I just had another beer"
   - "Actually I ate lunch at noon"
   - "My weight is actually 190"
   The BAC will recalculate immediately

4. DRINK TYPES RECOGNIZED
   Beer: light beer, IPA, stout
   Wine: red wine, white wine
   Spirits: whiskey, vodka, rum, gin, tequila
   Mixed: cocktail, margarita, cosmopolitan

5. TIME FORMATS UNDERSTOOD
   - "7pm" or "7:30pm" (24-hour: "19:30")
   - "2 hours ago"
   - "30 minutes ago"
   - "now" or "just now"
